# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@naar-noor.com**. All reports are reviewed promptly and confidentially. Community leaders may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Consequences

1. **Warning** — Private written warning with explanation
2. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary removal from community spaces
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal for severe violations
